# EcoPilot

> **AI-Powered Sustainability Intelligence Platform**

Version: 1.0
Status: Product Planning

---

# Vision

EcoPilot empowers organizations to measure, understand, and improve their environmental sustainability through intelligent analytics, AI-powered recommendations, and professional reporting.

Rather than relying on expensive IoT hardware or complex enterprise software, EcoPilot enables companies to use existing operational data—such as utility bills, printing records, and manually logged office activities—to gain meaningful sustainability insights.

The platform transforms raw environmental data into actionable business intelligence that helps organizations reduce operational costs while improving sustainability performance.

---

# Mission

To make sustainability management simple, affordable, and accessible for organizations of every size through artificial intelligence.

---

# Problem Statement

Most organizations want to become more environmentally responsible, but face several challenges:

* Sustainability data is scattered across spreadsheets, utility bills, and reports.
* Environmental performance is difficult to measure consistently.
* Small and medium-sized businesses cannot afford expensive ESG software.
* Employees lack visibility into how daily office activities affect sustainability.
* Decision-makers often receive reports too late to take meaningful action.
* Organizations struggle to identify practical opportunities to reduce operational costs while improving sustainability.

As a result, sustainability initiatives are often reactive rather than data-driven.

---

# Solution

EcoPilot centralizes sustainability information into one intelligent platform.

Organizations can:

* Track office resource consumption
* Upload utility bills and sustainability documents
* Monitor environmental performance
* Analyze trends over time
* Estimate carbon emissions
* Set sustainability goals
* Receive AI-generated recommendations
* Generate executive-ready sustainability reports

The platform acts as an AI Sustainability Copilot that continuously helps organizations make smarter environmental decisions.

---

# Core Value Proposition

EcoPilot transforms sustainability from a reporting task into a continuous decision-support system.

Instead of only displaying charts, the platform explains:

* What happened
* Why it happened
* What it means
* What should be done next

* Explain trends

* Recommend improvements
