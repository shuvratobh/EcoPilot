---

# 2. Vision Statement

EcoPilot enables organizations to measure, understand, and improve their environmental sustainability through intelligent analytics, AI-powered insights, and executive-ready reporting.

The platform transforms everyday operational data into actionable sustainability intelligence, helping organizations reduce costs, lower environmental impact, and make informed business decisions.

---

# 3. Mission Statement

To make sustainability management simple, intelligent, and affordable for every organization.

---

# 4. Problem Statement

Organizations generate large amounts of sustainability-related information, but much of it remains fragmented across utility bills, spreadsheets, manual reports, and disconnected systems.

As a result:

* Resource consumption is difficult to monitor.
* Sustainability reporting is time-consuming.
* Environmental trends often go unnoticed.
* Cost-saving opportunities are missed.
* Decision-makers lack timely, actionable insights.
* Many organizations cannot justify the cost or complexity of enterprise ESG software.

EcoPilot addresses these challenges by providing a centralized platform that combines analytics, AI, and reporting into a single, user-friendly experience.

---

# 5. Product Goals

The platform should help organizations:

* Monitor office resource consumption.
* Estimate environmental impact.
* Understand sustainability trends.
* Reduce operational costs.
* Improve sustainability performance.
* Generate professional sustainability reports.
* Support data-driven decision making.

---

# 6. Business Goals

Within the MVP, EcoPilot should demonstrate the ability to:

* Centralize sustainability data.
* Provide meaningful AI-generated recommendations.
* Reduce manual reporting effort.
* Improve visibility into environmental performance.
* Offer a polished SaaS experience suitable for commercial adoption.

# 13. Functional Requirements

The system must allow users to:

* Register and authenticate securely.
* Manage organizations and departments.
* Upload sustainability-related documents.
* Track office resource usage.
* Calculate sustainability metrics.
* Generate AI-powered recommendations.
* Create sustainability goals.
* Export reports.
* Manage users based on roles.

---

# 14. Non-Functional Requirements

The application must be:

* Responsive
* Accessible (WCAG AA where practical)
* Secure
* Scalable
* Maintainable
* Fast-loading
* Mobile-friendly
* SEO-friendly (marketing site)
* Well-documented
* Modular

---

# 15. Success Metrics (KPIs)

The MVP should demonstrate:

* Utility bill analysis completed in under 30 seconds.
* AI report generation completed in under 20 seconds.
* Dashboard loads in under 2 seconds on a typical broadband connection.
* Responsive layout across mobile, tablet, and desktop.
* Consistent design system across all pages.
* Zero critical accessibility issues.
* Reusable components used throughout the application.

---

# 16. Risks

Potential risks include:

* OCR inaccuracies on poor-quality documents.
* AI hallucinations if prompts are poorly designed.
* Users providing incomplete data.
* Scope expansion beyond the MVP.

Mitigation:

* Validate extracted data before saving.
* Keep AI grounded in uploaded data.
* Clearly define MVP boundaries.
* Build modularly to allow future expansion.

---

# 17. Assumptions

The MVP assumes:

* Users can manually enter missing data.
* Organizations have access to utility bills or reports.
* Internet connectivity is available for cloud-based AI services.
* Sustainability estimates are acceptable where exact measurements are unavailable.

---

# 18. Definition of Success

EcoPilot will be considered successful when it enables organizations to:

* Understand sustainability performance through a single dashboard.
* Identify opportunities to reduce environmental impact.
* Generate professional reports with minimal manual effort.
* Receive practical AI-powered recommendations.
* Monitor measurable progress toward sustainability goals.

The platform should leave users feeling informed, empowered, and confident in their sustainability decisions.

---

# 19. Long-Term Vision

EcoPilot should evolve into a comprehensive Sustainability Intelligence Platform that supports organizations in managing environmental performance through analytics, AI, and reporting.

Future enhancements should deepen sustainability capabilities rather than expanding into unrelated business domains.

Every new feature should reinforce the platform's mission of helping organizations make smarter environmental decisions.
